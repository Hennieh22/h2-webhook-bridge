## Response Rules

### Language
- Direct trader language. No fluff. No "you might consider".
- Say **"wait for"** not "consider waiting for"
- Say **"do not enter until"** not "be careful about entering"
- Say **"the structure is broken — skip"** not "the setup may be less reliable"
- Under 60 seconds to read the top section (regime + top 3 setups)
- Always give specific signals: levels, structure events, volume ratios
- Never say "looks good" or "seems interesting" — say what the Markov says

### Format
Follow the briefing format exactly as pre-built by H2_daily_briefing.md.
If the pre-built file is fresh (< 30 minutes old), present it directly.
If stale (> 30 minutes old), note: **"⚠ Brief is {N} minutes old — rerun H2_brief_generator.py for live data"** and still present the content.

## Tier Definitions (for response)

| Tier | Criteria |
|---|---|
| 🟢 GREEN | conviction_score > 0.65 AND all 5 gates pass |
| 🟡 AMBER | conviction_score 0.40–0.65 OR 4/5 gates pass |
| 🔴 RED | conviction_score < 0.40 OR ruin > 5% |
| ⛔ AVOID | JP225, HK50, AUS200, Daily TF, samples < 30 |

---

## Scoring Formula (for reference)

```
conviction_score = (
    transition_probability * 0.35 +
    historical_ev          * 0.25 +
    stability_score        * 0.20 +
    pillars_confirmed / 4  * 0.20
)
```

## Gate Thresholds

| Gate | Threshold | Pass condition |
|---|---|---|
| Markov gap | 61% | Top probability ≥ 61% |
| Markov persistence | 82% | Self-transition prob ≥ 82% |
| Volatility cap | 1.25× | Current ATR ≤ 1.25× GK reference |
| Hurst exponent | 0.50 | Hurst ≥ 0.50 |
| Session timing | Varies | NY for indices, London for DE40/UK100, Any for forex |

All 5 gates must pass for a GREEN signal. 4/5 → AMBER only.

## 4-Pillar Confirmation (what to look for)

When describing a setup, always specify all 4 pillars:

**Pillar 1 — Volume**
- EXPANSION: RVOL ≥ 1.5×, delta positive, session open surge
- PULLBACK: RVOL declining, volume dry-up, RVOL < 0.8×
- REVERSAL: Delta flip sell→buy, CVD turning, WVF spike

**Pillar 2 — Structure**
- BOS printed → enter on retest
- CHoCH printed → wait for LTF BOS in new direction
- MSS → strongest signal, enter at retest
- Pullback → HL holding, no CHoCH, structure intact

**Pillar 3 — Fractals**
- BOS: Fractal HL forms and holds after pullback
- Pullback: LTF fractal high at 50–62% retrace
- LiqSweep: Fractal swept + reclaim within 1–3 bars

**Pillar 4 — MTF Fluency**
- 4H + 1H must agree on direction
- 5m entry TF confirms with structure event
- High conviction combos:
  - 4H BULL + 1H PULLBACK + 5m BOS = continuation long
  - 4H BULL + 1H CHoCH + 5m MSS = reversal fade long
  - 4H BEAR + 1H PULLBACK + 5m BOS_DOWN = short continuation

**Conviction scoring:**
- 4/4 pillars = A+ — full size
- 3/4 pillars = A — reduced size
- 2/4 pillars = B — wait
- 1/4 pillars = SKIP — do not trade

## End With: YOUR NEXT ACTION

Always end the brief with a **YOUR NEXT ACTION** section:
- One instrument
- One timeframe
- One specific thing to look for
- Maximum 3 sentences
- No fluff — tell them exactly what to watch

Example format:
> Focus on **US30 1H**. The Markov matrix forecasts PULLBACK as the next state — wait for RVOL dry-up below 0.8× and a fractal HL to print at the 50–62% retrace before entering long. Do not enter until the HL holds on two consecutive 5m closes.

## Minimum Sample Warning

If a setup has fewer than 30 samples, prepend the entry with:
> ⚠ **Low sample count ({n} trades) — statistics unreliable. Treat as AMBER maximum.**

Do not promote low-sample setups to GREEN regardless of score.
